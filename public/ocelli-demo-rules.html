The problem & the fix

Why a single dashboard

😵 Today — scattered across tools

  • ✗ A Slack DM asks for your input — you see it, then forget
  • ✗ A Zendesk ticket is assigned to you — buried in noise
  • ✗ A Sheet row is due EOD — nobody reminded you
  • ✗ 6 apps open, no single view of "what needs me"
  • ✗ Things slip → missed deadlines, dropped balls

✨ With Ocelli — one console

  • ✓ Every tool feeds into one inbox
  • ✓ Each item auto-sorted: FYI / needs reply / to finish
  • ✓ Due dates detected automatically from the text
  • ✓ Tracked until done — snooze & it comes back
  • ✓ Your day, your tasks, your team's free time — at a glance

How it works — 3 steps

STEP 1 · INGEST

Connect your tools

Slack, Gmail, Zendesk, Sheets and Calendar feed items in automatically — filtered so only what's relevant to you arrives.

STEP 2 · TRIAGE

Auto-sort by rules

A transparent rules engine labels each item 🔵 FYI, 🟠 needs reply, or 🔴 to finish — and shows you why.

STEP 3 · TRACK

Nothing slips

Items stay until done. Snooze and they return next morning. Your morning brief sums up what matters.

Rules now · AI later

This demo = the rules engine. AI is an optional upgrade.

Everything you just saw runs on a transparent rules engine — no AI, nothing sent to any external service, works at zero cost. AI is a layer we can switch on later for richer help. (See the separate AI demo file for that.)

Why start with rules

Rules are private (no data leaves the company), instant, free, and transparent (you see why every item was sorted). We pilot with rules, prove the value, then turn on AI where it clearly helps — using a paid/private model so company data stays protected.
