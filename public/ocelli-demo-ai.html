What the AI layer adds

7 things AI does that rules can't

Rules sort and track reliably. AI adds judgment, language and drafting on top.

📝

Natural-language brief ✦

Reads everything and tells you what to do first, in plain English — not just counts.

✍️

Suggested replies ✦

One-click drafts in your tone, ready to review and send. Cuts reply time dramatically.

📚

Thread TL;DRs ✦

Collapses a 14-message email chain into the 3 lines that actually matter.

🎯

Priority ranking ✦

Decides what's truly #1 by deadline and who's blocked — with the reasoning shown.

🚨

Urgency & sentiment ✦

Spots the angry customer or the churn risk and flags it to escalate.

🧠

Context & recall ✦

"This matches 3 past tickets" — connects new items to history and known fixes.
